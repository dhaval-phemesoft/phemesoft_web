Content Development Process
  • Content Development from Scratch

    • We develop content for higher education, starting from the curriculum development. This involves delivering high quality content to bridge the gap between industry and academics. This refers to the cycle of content development which comprises of curriculum review by industry and academic experts.


Based on the review the curriculum is framed and content is developed by Subject Matter Experts in the respective domains.


  • Content of High Relevance

    • IBM has specialization programs which have content aligned with IT industry. The curriculum design is based on foundation, core and advanced subjects. The curriculum and courseware are developed by IBM labs, learning services teams and IBM partners. The content focuses on applied IT industry skills emphasising on labs and hands-on, The course content is also provided with links to reference materials for further study. Every course is provided with numerous case studies from industry. Inclusion of such case studies and industry scenarios provides the student with the opportunity to experience application of the theoretical aspect of the subject.


  • More on Practice Less on Theories

    • Every subject developed is supported by a laboratory on cloud, which gives rich hands on experience on the various tools used in the industry. The lab is supported by a simulation video to help the student work independently. The lab exercises include industry scenarios to be implemented, which orients the students to focus on project works.

Quality Assurance

Every step involves an effective quality assurance process.

Our Company believes in two objectives:

  • Customer delight
  • Zero defect

Customer delight is achieved through “Timely delivery”. To achieve this a review mechanism and strategy is followed. The QA process consists of:


  • Intra Functional Review

    • The intra functional review team is to ensure the content received from the SME is run through a plagiarism check to ensure that it is plagiarism free. Further the content is checked for readability, applicability and relevance to the curriculum.


  • Technical Review

    • The Subject Matter Experts and industry veterans undertake a technical review to ensure the content meets the global standards, covers latest changes in the technology and industry, provides more experiential learning.


  • External/Customer Review

    • The content is then passed on to the customer or any external bodies identified for a final relevancy, sufficiency and industry applicability check.

Presentation Layer

The developed industry relevant and emerging technology content is available to the student and teacher not only in the form of traditional books but also in the form of online flip books, interactive videos which can be accessed from gadgets that are ubiquitous in the life of students, like tablets and phones. The content developed are in the form of interactive digital content which keeps the student engaged in the subject.


  • Flip Books

    • The flip books developed are interactive to the student where they can migrate to various external links to avail more information on the topic of interest. The book also has videos by SME and animation videos embedded for better understanding of the concept by the student. At the end of every course unit the student is provided with a questionnaire to asses them on the subject.


  • Audio Visual

    • The concept-based AV developed emphasises on the concepts for the student. The AV developed consists of animation, videos and explanation by SME for better understanding of the concepts. The student can access the AV from the gadget of their choice.


  • PDF Books

    • The content developed are published as student guides for reference by the students. Each unit has a set of assessment questions for student self-evaluation.


  • Lab Guides

    • The content for the lab exercises are available to the student as student exercise guides. This consists of experiments related to the industry case studies.
