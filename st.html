Curriculum workshop
  • The content developed will be aligned with IT industry.
  • The content progression will be gradual and designed to cover foundation, core and advanced subjects.
  • The courseware is developed by team of industry veterans and Subject matter experts to ensure the relevancy of content.

Capability development program for faculty
  • Capability development program for faculty will be delivered before the commencement of the semester – usually at the skill park/university
  • The content will be available on the Knowbots portal
  • Assessment test will be conducted for the faculty to evaluate their understanding on the course
  • Rich experiential training on various tools for the courses offered

Industry guest lectures
  • SMEs (Subject Matter Experts) on each technology/domain will visit the skill park/spokes and deliver guest lectures to students
  • Webinars are conducted regularly to connect the educators with the educated
  • These guest lectures are part of the specialization courses which are embedded in the curriculum
  • The SMEs also available for interaction with the students as a mentor, through the platform while doing the projects

Content delivery
  • The content will be delivered both physically and through online portal
  • The rich content and experiential learning guide will be provided for all the students
  • A virtual classes and virtual labs capability is offered to enhance the quality of learning and are available remotely to access
